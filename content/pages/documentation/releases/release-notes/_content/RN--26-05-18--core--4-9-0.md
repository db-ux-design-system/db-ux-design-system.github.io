### Design

- feat: slot integration (part 1) for following components:
  - Accordion, Notification: _Children_ slot below text
  - Badge (Text), Button, Link (Auto Width), Infotext (Auto Width), Tooltip (Auto Width): _Children_ slot right to text
  - Checkbox, Radio, Switch: _Children_ slot right to label
  - Tag: _Content_ slot right to icon, _Children_ slot right to text
