- Position Top: Horizontal navigation bar with Popover navigation, Brand, Actions, and optional Meta bar
- Position Left: Vertical sidebar with Popover, Tree, or FlatIcon navigation and expand/collapse behavior
- Width property (Top only): Full, Small, Medium, Large — controls the max-width of the content area
- Supports Brand, PrimaryActions, SecondaryActions, Meta, and Navigation subcomponents

## Guidelines

<CollapsibleWrapper collapsedHeight="320px">

### Position (Top vs. Left)

Choose the ControlPanel position based on your application's navigation complexity. Top works for apps with few top-level items; Left suits applications with many items or deep hierarchies.

## Examples

<CollapsibleWrapper collapsedHeight="320px">

### Navigation Variant (Left)

The Left position supports three navigation variants: Popover for flat-to-medium hierarchies, Tree for deep structures with 3+ levels, and FlatIcon for quick icon-based access to a small set of destinations.

<div className="guideline-examples">
	<GuidelineExample
		figmaUrl="https://embed.figma.com/design/pElrqVUyojrzYzSagyJPS6/Doku--ControlPanel?node-id=4042-6&embed-host=share"
		description="Popover for navigation with fewer items and shallow hierarchy (up to 2 levels), or Tree for larger structures with 3+ levels of nesting."
	/>
	<GuidelineExample
		figmaUrl="https://embed.figma.com/design/pElrqVUyojrzYzSagyJPS6/Doku--ControlPanel?node-id=4042-8&embed-host=share"
		description="FlatIcon for a small number of equally weighted top-level destinations with clear, recognizable icons."
	/>
</div>

### Expand/Collapse (Left)

The Left ControlPanel can be collapsed via a slide button to save screen space. It is always initially expanded. In the collapsed state, the navigation content is hidden and only the Brand and slide button remain visible.
