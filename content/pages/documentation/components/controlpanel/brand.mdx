## Guidelines

<CollapsibleWrapper collapsedHeight="320px">

### Custom Brand Override

The Custom variant allows placing your own brand/logo via the Children slot. The Short variant is system-controlled and shows the default compact brand mark in constrained contexts.

	<GuidelineExample

		description="Override the Custom variant with your application logo for expanded ControlPanel states (Top, Left expanded, Mobile Top)."

	<GuidelineExample
		variant="dont"
		figmaUrl="https://embed.figma.com/design/pElrqVUyojrzYzSagyJPS6/Doku--ControlPanel?node-id=4185-4&embed-host=share"
		description="Do not attempt to override the Short variant. It is automatically applied in collapsed Left and Mobile Bottom positions and is not user-configurable."
	/>
</div>

### Logo Addition

The application name next to the logo must be an official logo addition provided as an approved SVG (vectorized paths), requested via the marketing portal. This ensures brand consistency in font, spacing, and rendering.

<div className="guideline-examples">
	<GuidelineExample
		variant="do"
		figmaUrl="https://embed.figma.com/design/pElrqVUyojrzYzSagyJPS6/Doku--ControlPanel?node-id=4252-87&embed-host=share"
		description="Use an approved SVG where logo and text addition form a single vectorized asset."
	/>
	<GuidelineExample
		variant="dont"
		figmaUrl="https://embed.figma.com/design/pElrqVUyojrzYzSagyJPS6/Doku--ControlPanel?node-id=4252-97&embed-host=share"
		description="Do not combine the default logo with your own text element. Custom text deviates from the brand in font, spacing, and rendering."
	/>
</div>

### Brand with FlatIcon

The FlatIcon ControlPanel has no dedicated Brand area. If brand visibility is required, place the logo in the Shell Content area instead.

<div className="guideline-examples">
	<GuidelineExample
		variant="do"
		figmaUrl="https://embed.figma.com/design/pElrqVUyojrzYzSagyJPS6/Doku--ControlPanel?node-id=4185-6&embed-host=share"
		description="Place the brand/logo in the Shell Content area (e.g. as a header) when using FlatIcon and brand presence is needed."
	/>
	<GuidelineExample
		variant="dont"
		figmaUrl="https://embed.figma.com/design/pElrqVUyojrzYzSagyJPS6/Doku--ControlPanel?node-id=4185-8&embed-host=share"
		description="Do not place a brand/logo as a FlatIcon NavigationItem. The tab bar is reserved for navigation destinations only."
	/>
</div>

### Brand Slot Content

The Brand slot is for identification and context. Do not place interactive elements in it – actions and inputs belong in PrimaryActions or SecondaryActions.

<div className="guideline-examples">
	<GuidelineExample
		variant="do"

		description="Use the Brand slot for supplementary context like an environment badge (e.g. Staging, Beta) alongside the logo."
