## Guidelines

<CollapsibleWrapper collapsedHeight="320px">

### Action Selection

Only place truly primary, high-frequency actions in this slot. Keep the number minimal to avoid clutter in the ControlPanel bar.

<div className="guideline-examples">
	<GuidelineExample
		variant="do"
		figmaUrl="https://embed.figma.com/design/pElrqVUyojrzYzSagyJPS6/Doku--ControlPanel?node-id=TODO-do&embed-host=share"
		description="Place 1–3 high-frequency actions (e.g. search, notifications) that users need on every page."
	/>
	<GuidelineExample
		variant="dont"
		figmaUrl="https://embed.figma.com/design/pElrqVUyojrzYzSagyJPS6/Doku--ControlPanel?node-id=TODO-dont&embed-host=share"
		description="Do not fill the PrimaryActions slot with many buttons. Too many actions dilute priority and clutter the ControlPanel."
	/>
</div>

### FlatIcon Limitation

The FlatIcon ControlPanel does not support PrimaryActions. If your application requires always-visible primary actions, use Top or Bottom position instead.

<div className="guideline-examples">
	<GuidelineExample
