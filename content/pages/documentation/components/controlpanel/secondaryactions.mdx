## Guidelines

<CollapsibleWrapper collapsedHeight="320px">

### Action Type

SecondaryActions hold supporting utility actions that are relevant across all pages but are not the user's primary task. Keep them visually subordinate to the navigation and PrimaryActions.

<div className="guideline-examples">
	<GuidelineExample
		variant="do"
		figmaUrl="https://embed.figma.com/design/pElrqVUyojrzYzSagyJPS6/Doku--ControlPanel?node-id=4354-2&embed-host=share"
		description="Place utility actions (e.g. user profile, settings, help) that users may need occasionally."
	/>
	<GuidelineExample
		variant="dont"
		figmaUrl="https://embed.figma.com/design/pElrqVUyojrzYzSagyJPS6/Doku--ControlPanel?node-id=4354-4&embed-host=share"
		description="Do not place frequently used actions (e.g. search, notifications) in SecondaryActions. These belong in PrimaryActions where they are immediately visible."
	/>
</div>

### Count

Keep SecondaryActions minimal. Too many icons at the end of the ControlPanel create visual noise and compete with the navigation.

<div className="guideline-examples">
	<GuidelineExample
		variant="do"
		figmaUrl="https://embed.figma.com/design/pElrqVUyojrzYzSagyJPS6/Doku--ControlPanel?node-id=4354-6&embed-host=share"
		description="Use 1–3 utility actions that support the user without cluttering the navigation bar."
	/>
	<GuidelineExample
		variant="dont"
		figmaUrl="https://embed.figma.com/design/pElrqVUyojrzYzSagyJPS6/Doku--ControlPanel?node-id=4354-8&embed-host=share"
		description="Do not overload SecondaryActions with too many icons. A crowded end area makes the ControlPanel feel heavy and unfocused."
	/>
</div>

### FlatIcon Limitation

The FlatIcon ControlPanel does not support SecondaryActions. Utility actions like profile or settings should be placed as FlatIcon NavigationItems or moved into a dedicated page.

<div className="guideline-examples">
	<GuidelineExample
		variant="do"
		figmaUrl="https://embed.figma.com/design/pElrqVUyojrzYzSagyJPS6/Doku--ControlPanel?node-id=4354-10&embed-host=share"
		description="Use Top or Bottom ControlPanel when secondary utility actions need a dedicated slot separated from navigation."
	/>
	<GuidelineExample
		variant="dont"
		figmaUrl="https://embed.figma.com/design/pElrqVUyojrzYzSagyJPS6/Doku--ControlPanel?node-id=4354-12&embed-host=share"
		description="Do not mix utility actions with navigation destinations in FlatIcon. Account or Settings can be a NavigationItem, but not an action button."
	/>
</div>

</CollapsibleWrapper>
