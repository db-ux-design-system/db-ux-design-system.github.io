### Use badges for supplementary information

Badges should provide additional context and must not carry essential information on their own.

## FAQ

<DBAccordion>
	<DBAccordionItem headlinePlain="Can a badge be interactive?">
		No, badges are non-interactive. If interaction is required, use a Tag or Button instead.
	</DBAccordionItem>
	<DBAccordionItem headlinePlain="How many badges can be shown together?">
		Use badges sparingly. Too many badges reduce readability and visual clarity.
	</DBAccordionItem>
	<DBAccordionItem headlinePlain="Can icons be used inside badges?">
		Only if they support the text label and do not replace it.
	</DBAccordionItem>
</DBAccordion>
