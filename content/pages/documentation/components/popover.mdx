## Guidelines

<CollapsibleWrapper collapsedHeight="320px">

###### Popover vs Tooltip

Use Popover when content is related to a specific trigger and users may need to interact with it. Use Tooltip for brief, non-interactive hints on hover/focus.

<div className="guideline-examples">
	<GuidelineExample
		variant="do"
		figmaUrl="https://embed.figma.com/design/JNtTZmwnMHbHVeh0FhcO1c/Doku--Popover-%E2%80%BC%EF%B8%8F?node-id=1-1132&embed-host=share"
		description="Use a popover for a set of related options anchored to an object."
	/>
	<GuidelineExample
		variant="dont"
		figmaUrl="https://embed.figma.com/design/JNtTZmwnMHbHVeh0FhcO1c/Doku--Popover-%E2%80%BC%EF%B8%8F?node-id=1-1133&embed-host=share"
		description="Don't use a popover for displaying the label of a icon button (use tooltip instead)."
	/>
</div>

###### Content

Popover content should be concise and scannable. Prefer a clear title (if needed), short text, and a small number of actions. Avoid dense layouts.

<div className="guideline-examples">
	<GuidelineExample
		variant="do"
		figmaUrl="https://embed.figma.com/design/JNtTZmwnMHbHVeh0FhcO1c/Doku--Popover-%E2%80%BC%EF%B8%8F?node-id=3-1110&embed-host=share"
		description="Present one topic with a clear hierarchy and a limited number of controls."
	/>
	<GuidelineExample
		variant="dont"
		figmaUrl="https://embed.figma.com/design/JNtTZmwnMHbHVeh0FhcO1c/Doku--Popover-%E2%80%BC%EF%B8%8F?node-id=3-1112&embed-host=share"
		description="Don't pack multiple unrelated sections or large, scroll-heavy content into a popover."
	/>
</div>

###### Placement

Choose a placement that keeps the popover near its trigger and within the viewport. Allow auto-flip when near edges and avoid covering the trigger or key content.

<div className="guideline-examples">
	<GuidelineExample
		variant="do"
		figmaUrl="https://embed.figma.com/design/JNtTZmwnMHbHVeh0FhcO1c/Doku--Popover-%E2%80%BC%EF%B8%8F?node-id=3-1113&embed-host=share"
		description="Use default placement and enable flipping to keep the popover fully visible."

		description="Don't force a placement that causes clipping, overlaps critical UI, or hides the trigger."
