Popover is a floating container anchored to a trigger that reveals additional, contextual content on click or hover. It supports richer content than a tooltip and can include actions, but should remain lightweight and easy to dismiss.

- Provide contextual actions or settings near a control (e.g., "More options", quick preferences)
- Show short explanatory content with links or small controls (e.g., help + "Learn more")
- Display compact, interactive content without navigating away (e.g., quick filters)

## Usage Hints

### Popover vs Tooltip

Use popover when content is related to a specific trigger and users may need to interact with it. Use Tooltip for brief, non-interactive hints on hover/focus.

### Content

Popover content should be concise and scannable. Prefer a clear title (if needed), short text, and a small number of actions. Avoid dense layouts.

### Placement

Choose a placement that keeps the popover near its trigger and within the viewport. Allow auto-flip when near edges and avoid covering the trigger or key content.
