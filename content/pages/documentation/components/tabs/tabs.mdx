## Guidelines

<CollapsibleWrapper collapsedHeight="320px">

### Content

Tabs represent different aspects of the same subject at the same hierarchy level. They are not a substitute for page-level navigation between unrelated destinations.

### Active State

Only one tab is active at a time. The active tab displays its corresponding panel while all other panels are hidden.
