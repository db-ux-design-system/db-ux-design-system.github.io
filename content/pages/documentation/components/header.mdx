## Guidelines

<CollapsibleWrapper collapsedHeight="320px">

###### Consistent structure

Use the standard order: Brand section with logo and name, primary navigation, then actions. Add meta navigation only when it serves a distinct, secondary purpose and does not compete with primary navigation.

<div className="guideline-examples">
	<GuidelineExample
		variant="do"
		figmaUrl="https://embed.figma.com/design/SAbMEPHY6sZfqYvL2OPuk9/Doku--Header-%E2%80%BC%EF%B8%8F?node-id=1-1132&embed-host=share"
		description="Place logo on the left, primary navigation in center or right, actions grouped at the far right."
	/>
	<GuidelineExample
		variant="dont"
		figmaUrl="https://embed.figma.com/design/SAbMEPHY6sZfqYvL2OPuk9/Doku--Header-%E2%80%BC%EF%B8%8F?node-id=1-1133&embed-host=share"
		description="Scatter logo, navigation, and actions randomly, or place meta navigation more prominently than primary navigation."
	/>
</div>

###### Navigation vs. actions

Primary navigation is for destinations; actions are for immediate operations like search, user menu, or settings. Keep actions visually grouped and avoid duplicating destinations as actions.

<div className="guideline-examples">
	<GuidelineExample
		variant="do"
		figmaUrl="https://embed.figma.com/design/SAbMEPHY6sZfqYvL2OPuk9/Doku--Header-%E2%80%BC%EF%B8%8F?node-id=3-1113&embed-host=share"
		description="Use icon buttons for actions and keep destination links in the navigation area."
	/>
	<GuidelineExample
		variant="dont"
		figmaUrl="https://embed.figma.com/design/SAbMEPHY6sZfqYvL2OPuk9/Doku--Header-%E2%80%BC%EF%B8%8F?node-id=3-1114&embed-host=share"
		description="Put navigation destinations into the action area or mix actions into the nav list."
	/>
</div>

###### Mark external links

If the meta navigation contains external links, communicate that they leave the application with label or icon as defined and ensure the behavior is consistent.

<div className="guideline-examples">
	<GuidelineExample
		variant="do"
		figmaUrl="https://embed.figma.com/design/SAbMEPHY6sZfqYvL2OPuk9/Doku--Header-%E2%80%BC%EF%B8%8F?node-id=3-1110&embed-host=share"
		description="Mark external destinations clearly and keep the interaction consistent across the header."
	/>
	<GuidelineExample
		variant="dont"
		figmaUrl="https://embed.figma.com/design/SAbMEPHY6sZfqYvL2OPuk9/Doku--Header-%E2%80%BC%EF%B8%8F?node-id=3-1112&embed-host=share"
		description="Hide external behavior or mix external and internal links without clear indication."
	/>
</div>
