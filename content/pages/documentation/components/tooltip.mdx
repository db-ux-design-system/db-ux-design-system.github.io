Tooltip provides brief, contextual information on hover or focus without interrupting the user flow. It is best for clarifying icons, truncated text, or unfamiliar UI controls, and should not contain critical or lengthy content.

- Explain the meaning of an icon-only control (e.g., "Download", "Copy link")
- Reveal truncated text or labels (e.g., full file name, full table header)
- Add short hints for optional inputs or uncommon terms

## Guidelines

<CollapsibleWrapper collapsedHeight="320px">

###### Usage

Tooltips display contextual information and must not contain required instructions or critical information needed to complete a task. If users must read it to proceed, use infotext, inline guidance, or a popover instead.

	/>
	<GuidelineExample

		description="Don't put mandatory form instructions or key policy text into a tooltip."
	/>
</div>

###### Content

Tooltip content should be one short sentence or phrase. Avoid links, multiple actions, or long paragraphs. If content needs structure or interaction, use a popover/dialog.

<div className="guideline-examples">
	<GuidelineExample
		variant="do"

		description="Use concise wording and plain language."
	/>
	<GuidelineExample
		variant="dont"
		figmaUrl="https://embed.figma.com/design/OBFhxnOw4VTa8sP9Wz36p4/Doku--Tooltip-%E2%80%BC%EF%B8%8F?node-id=3-1112&embed-host=share"
		description="Don't use multi-line explanations, lists, or interactive content inside a tooltip."
	/>
</div>

###### Trigger

Show the tooltip on hover and keyboard focus. Use a small delay to prevent flicker, and hide it on blur, mouse leave, scroll, or Escape. Do not require precision hovering.

<div className="guideline-examples">
	<GuidelineExample

		description="Trigger on hover/focus, dismiss on blur/leave/Escape."

	<GuidelineExample
		variant="dont"

		description="Don't show tooltips only on hover (keyboard users miss them)."
	/>
</div>

###### Placement

Place tooltips close to the trigger and avoid covering what the user is trying to read or click. Auto-flip when near viewport edges and keep an appropriate offset.
