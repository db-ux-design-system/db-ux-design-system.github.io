## Guidelines

<CollapsibleWrapper collapsedHeight="320px">

### Multi-Level Rule

Multi-level navigation (Popover or Tree) can exist in either the ControlPanel or the SubNavigation. Applying them in both creates conflicting navigation patterns.

<div className="guideline-examples">
	<GuidelineExample
		variant="do"
		figmaUrl="https://embed.figma.com/design/RcpbbqfJwjNRlOwPhAX1im/Doku--Shell?node-id=4093-2&embed-host=share"
		description="Use multi-level navigation (Popover or Tree) in the SubNavigation only when the ControlPanel has a single-level structure."
	/>
	<GuidelineExample
		variant="dont"
		figmaUrl="https://embed.figma.com/design/RcpbbqfJwjNRlOwPhAX1im/Doku--Shell?node-id=4093-5&embed-host=share"
		description="Do not use multi-level navigation in both the ControlPanel and the SubNavigation simultaneously."
	/>
</div>

### Mobile Limitation

The mobile SubNavigation supports only one navigation level. Keep the structure flat for touch-friendly access.

<div className="guideline-examples">
	<GuidelineExample
		variant="do"
		figmaUrl="https://embed.figma.com/design/RcpbbqfJwjNRlOwPhAX1im/Doku--Shell?node-id=4093-8&embed-host=share"
		description="Use a single level of navigation items in the mobile SubNavigation."
	/>
	<GuidelineExample
		variant="dont"
		figmaUrl="https://embed.figma.com/design/RcpbbqfJwjNRlOwPhAX1im/Doku--Shell?node-id=4093-11&embed-host=share"
		description="Do not overload the mobile SubNavigation with too many items or nested levels. Only one navigation level is supported and excessive horizontal scrolling reduces discoverability."
	/>
</div>

### Variant (Popover vs. Tree)

Choose the navigation variant based on your content hierarchy. Popover works for flat structures; Tree works for deep, expandable page trees.

<div className="guideline-examples">
	<GuidelineExample
		variant="do"
		figmaUrl="https://embed.figma.com/design/RcpbbqfJwjNRlOwPhAX1im/Doku--Shell?node-id=4093-14&embed-host=share"
		description="Use Popover for flat navigation with few items and Tree for hierarchical structures with nested pages."
	/>
	<GuidelineExample
		variant="dont"
		figmaUrl="https://embed.figma.com/design/RcpbbqfJwjNRlOwPhAX1im/Doku--Shell?node-id=4093-17&embed-host=share"
		description="Do not use Popover for deeply nested page structures. Users lose their position in the hierarchy when the Popover closes after each interaction."
	/>
</div>
