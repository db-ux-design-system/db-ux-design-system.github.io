## Guidelines

Choose the variant that fits your navigation structure:

- **Popover:** Flat to medium structures; reach deeper levels with a single click
- **Tree:** Complex hierarchies (3+ levels) where the full structure stays visible
- **DrillDown:** Many items with shallow depth (1–2 levels), shown one level at a time

### Horizontal Overflow

The horizontal SubNavigation has limited space. Too many items require scrolling which reduces discoverability – especially on mobile where no scroll indicators are shown.

### Mobile: Single Level

The mobile SubNavigation supports only one flat navigation level. Nested groups are not rendered.
