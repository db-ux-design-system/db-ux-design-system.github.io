## Guidelines

###### Fixed Spacing

Fixed spacings are gaps that remain the same regardless of screen size. They are typically used within components and modules to ensure a uniform and clear structure across different devices.

###### Responsive Spacing

Responsive spacings are gaps that automatically adapt to the size of the device screen (desktop, tablet, mobile). They are ideal for separating different layout areas and ensure a consistent, user-friendly appearance across all devices.
